🪐 Mercury

The Fastest and Smallest Planet

📖 About Mercury
Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and the smallest planet in our Solar System. It is a rocky world covered with thousands of craters, making it look similar to the Moon.

Because it is so close to the Sun, Mercury experiences extreme conditions — extremely hot during the day and freezing cold at night due to almost no atmosphere.

One full revolution around the Sun takes only 88 Earth days, making Mercury the fastest planet in the Solar System.

Even though it is closest to the Sun, Mercury is not the hottest planet — Venus is hotter due to its thick atmosphere.
🌌 10 Interesting Facts about Mercury

Mercury Facts

1. Mercury is the smallest planet in the Solar System.

2. It is the closest planet to the Sun.

3. Mercury has no atmosphere to trap heat.

4. It has extreme temperature differences.

5. A year on Mercury is only 88 Earth days.

6. Its surface is full of craters.

7. Mercury has no moons or rings.

8. It rotates very slowly on its axis.

9. Mercury is visible near sunrise or sunset.

10. It is not the hottest planet in the Solar System.
