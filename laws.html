📘 Newton’s Laws (Complete Theory)

🧠 What is Motion?

Motion is the change in position of an object with respect to time. In simple words, when anything moves from one place to another, it is called motion.

गति वह प्रक्रिया है जिसमें कोई वस्तु समय के साथ अपनी स्थिति बदलती है।

⚡ Types of Motion

  • Rectilinear Motion (सीधी रेखा में गति)
  • Circular Motion (वृत्ताकार गति)
  • Oscillatory Motion (दोलन गति)
  • Random Motion (अनियमित गति)

📘 Newton’s First Law

An object remains in rest or uniform motion unless acted upon by an external force.

कोई वस्तु तब तक स्थिर या समान गति में रहती है जब तक उस पर बाहरी बल न लगे।

📘 Newton’s Second Law

Force = Mass × Acceleration

Force depends on mass and acceleration. More mass needs more force.

📘 Newton’s Third Law

For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction.

हर क्रिया की बराबर और विपरीत प्रतिक्रिया होती है।

⚡ Important Physics Terms

  • Force = Push or Pull
  • Mass = Amount of matter
  • Acceleration = Change in velocity
  • Velocity = Speed with direction
  • Friction = Opposes motion
  • Gravity = Earth attraction force
  • Momentum = mass × velocity
  • Energy = ability to do work
