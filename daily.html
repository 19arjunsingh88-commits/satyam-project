🧴 Daily Use Chemicals

Chemicals we use in daily life (Home & Environment)

Water — H₂O
Used in drinking, cleaning, cooking
Sodium Chloride (Salt) — NaCl
Used in food seasoning
Sugar (Sucrose) — C₁₂H₂₂O₁₁
Used in sweet foods
Vinegar (Acetic Acid) — CH₃COOH
Used in cooking & cleaning
Baking Soda — NaHCO₃
Used in baking & cleaning
Washing Soda — Na₂CO₃
Bleaching Powder — CaOCl₂
Soap (Sodium Stearate) — C₁₇H₃₅COONa
Detergent — R-SO₄Na
Toothpaste (Fluoride compounds) — NaF / SnF₂
Milk — Complex mixture
Lemon Juice (Citric Acid) — C₆H₈O₇
Ammonia (Cleaning agent) — NH₃
Hydrogen Peroxide — H₂O₂
Ethanol (Sanitizer) — C₂H₅OH
Nail Polish Remover — Acetone (CH₃COCH₃)
Air (Oxygen) — O₂
Carbon Dioxide — CO₂
Chlorine (Disinfection) — Cl₂
Caffeine — C₈H₁₀N₄O₂
