📖 About Uranus
Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and one of the coldest and most unusual planets in the Solar System.

It has a beautiful blue-green color due to methane gas in its atmosphere, which absorbs red light and reflects blue-green tones.

Uranus is unique because it rotates on its side, making its axis highly tilted compared to other planets.

It is an ice giant with extremely cold temperatures and a mysterious atmosphere.
