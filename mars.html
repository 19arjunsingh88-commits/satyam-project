🔴 Mars

The Red Planet of the Future

📖 About Mars
Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is known as the Red Planet because of iron-rich dust on its surface. It is a cold desert world with mountains, valleys, and ancient riverbeds.

Mars has the largest volcano in the Solar System called Olympus Mons, which is nearly three times taller than Mount Everest. Scientists believe Mars once had rivers and lakes in the past.

Today, Mars is being explored by advanced robots like NASA’s Perseverance Rover to study its surface and search for signs of ancient life.
