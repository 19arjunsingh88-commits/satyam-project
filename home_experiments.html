🔋 Voltage Booster

BC547 transistor used to increase voltage.

🔌 Wiring:

- Battery + ➜ Transformer Primary +
- Battery - ➜ BC547 Emitter

- BC547 Collector ➜ Transformer Primary -
- Transformer Secondary ➜ Output LED +
- LED - ➜ Battery -

⚠️ Base of BC547 ➜ 1K resistor ➜ Battery +

💡 LED Switch Circuit

- Battery + ➜ Switch ➜ LED +
- LED - ➜ Resistor (220Ω) ➜ Battery -

🔊 Buzzer Alarm

- Battery + ➜ Buzzer +
- Buzzer - ➜ BC547 Collector
- BC547 Emitter ➜ Battery -
- Switch ➜ Base (through 1K resistor)

🌡️ Temperature Sensor

- Battery + ➜ Thermistor +
- Thermistor ➜ BC547 Base
- BC547 Emitter ➜ Battery -
- Collector ➜ LED +
- LED - ➜ Battery -
