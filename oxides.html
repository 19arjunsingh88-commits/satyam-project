⚗️ Oxides

Important metal and non-metal oxides with uses

Water (Hydrogen Oxide) — H₂O
✔ Essential for life, drinking, agriculture
Carbon Dioxide — CO₂
✔ Used in photosynthesis, fire extinguishers
Carbon Monoxide — CO
✔ Fuel gas (toxic, dangerous in high amount)
Sulfur Dioxide — SO₂
✔ Used in bleaching and food preservation
Sulfur Trioxide — SO₃
✔ Used in manufacturing sulfuric acid
Nitrogen Monoxide — NO
✔ Helps in biological signaling in body
Nitrogen Dioxide — NO₂
✔ Air pollution gas, acid rain formation
Calcium Oxide (Quick Lime) — CaO
✔ Used in cement, construction, soil treatment
Magnesium Oxide — MgO
✔ Used in refractory materials, medicine
Zinc Oxide — ZnO
✔ Used in sunscreen, ointments
Iron(II) Oxide — FeO
✔ Used in steel industry
Iron(III) Oxide (Rust) — Fe₂O₃
✔ Used in paints, magnets, rust formation
Aluminium Oxide — Al₂O₃
✔ Used in abrasives, ceramics
Copper Oxide — CuO
✔ Used in batteries and pigments
Lead Oxide — PbO
✔ Used in glass and ceramics industry

💡 Oxides are formed when elements combine with oxygen
